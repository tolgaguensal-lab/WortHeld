# CEFR German Content Requirements

Based on **Goethe-Institut** official standards:

## 📊 Official Teaching Hours (45-min units)

| Level | Units | Hours (cumulative) | What you can do |
|-------|-------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **A1** | 80-200 | 60-150h | Basic phrases, simple interactions |
| **A2** | 200-350 | 150-200h additional | Everyday expressions, routine tasks |
| **B1** | 350-650 | 150-250h additional | Handle most situations, explain opinions |
| **B2** | 600-800 | 200-250h additional | Complex texts, fluent interaction |
| **C1** | 800-1000 | 200-300h additional | Academic/professional German |

## 📚 Content Distribution (Recommended)

### A1 (Elementary) - 80-200 units
- **Vocabulary**: 200-300 words
  - Numbers, colors, family, food, basic objects
  - Greetings, simple verbs, adjectives
- **Grammar**: 10-15 topics
  - Articles (der/die/das)
  - Basic verb conjugation (sein, haben)
  - Present tense regular verbs
  - Simple sentence structure
  - Personal pronouns
  - Negation (nicht/kein)

### A2 (Elementary) - 150-200 additional units
- **Vocabulary**: 300-400 words
  - Daily routines, shopping, travel
  - Weather, time, directions
  - Work, hobbies, describing people
- **Grammar**: 15-20 topics
  - Perfect tense
  - Dative case introduction
  - Modal verbs (können, müssen, wollen)
  - Comparative/superlative
  - Separable verbs
  - Prepositions with cases

### B1 (Intermediate) - 150-250 additional units  
- **Vocabulary**: 400-500 words
  - Abstract concepts, opinions
  - Media, culture, society
  - Work, education, health
- **Grammar**: 20-25 topics
  - Past tense (Präteritum)
  - Genitive case
  - Passive voice
  - Konjunktiv II (conditional)
  - Relative clauses
  - Complex sentence structures

### B2 (Upper Intermediate) - 200-250 additional units
- **Vocabulary**: 500-600 words
  - Academic/professional terminology
  - Idioms, expressions
  - Specialized topics (business, science)
- **Grammar**: 20-25 topics
  - Konjunktiv I (reported speech)
  - Advanced passive constructions
  - Nominalization
  - Complex prepositional phrases
  - Stylistic variations

### C1 (Advanced) - 200-300 additional units
- **Vocabulary**: 600-700 words
  - Nuanced expressions
  - Academic writing
  - Literary language
- **Grammar**: 15-20 topics
  - Rhetorical devices
  - Formal register
  - Complex syntactic structures
  - Idiomatic usage
  - Register variation
